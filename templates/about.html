About Resale Value Predictor

About This Project

Curious what your HDB flat is really worth? Whether you're selling for top dollar or hunting for a fair deal, Resale Value Predictor delivers instant, data‑backed estimates based on your flat's specifications

How It Works

🔍 We source every HDB resale transaction since 2017 from the Housing and Development Board

🤖 Our LightGBM regression model uncovers the hidden factors that truly drive prices

🔄 Each week, our automated pipeline pulls in new sales data and retrains the model — so your valuations stay sharp

☁️ Your flat's details are forwarded to our REST API hosted on Google Cloud to obtain resale price predictions

🔗 Developers can also integrate directly: call our API here

Parameters Used for Modelling

  • 🏠 Flat Type
  • 🏢 Storey
  • 📏 Floor Area
  • ⏳ Remaining Lease
  • 🚶‍♂️ Walking Distance to Nearest MRT
  • 🛍️ Walking Distance to Nearest Mall
  • 🏫 Within 1km of Oversubscribed Primary School
  • 📈 Resale Price Index (RPI)
  • 🌳 Maturity of Estate
